About Me

Hi! I'm Jonya and welcome to my personal wesbite.

I'm a senior at Cornell studying ECE (Electrical and Computer Engineering), with a minor in Computer Science. On campus, I'm also very involved around the Cornell Engineering community, as president of a professional engineering fraternity (Theta Tau) and co-president of Cornell Engineering Ambassadors.

When I'm not completing my homework in Carpenter Library or hanging out in Duffield Hall (the engineering hub), you might find me with hanging out with my roommates and friends. I also love trying out new foods and taking pictures of them (hehe) so you can find those all in the Food Journal section of my website.

My main goal is to use my technical background to create engaging and meaningful user experiences.

I have a passion for developing product knowledge and design experience, all through hands-on approaches. In fact, the very website you're viewing now was a side project I started in order to teach myself HTML/CSS and learn how to utilize various web development tools. I'm constantly working to learn and improve, so feel free to give me feedback while you're browsing!
