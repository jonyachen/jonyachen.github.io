ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Jonya and welcome to my personal wesbite.

I'm currently a student at UC Berkeley, pursuing a one-year Master of Engineering program. I'm in the Electrical Engineering & Computer Science (EECS) department, with a concentration in visual computing and graphics. My current research project is focused on designing and building a software user interface to perform environmental assessment for industry under Prof. Bjoern Hartmann.

I completed my undergrad studies at Cornell University, with a major in Electrical and Computer Engineering and a minor in Computer Science. On campus, I was very involved around the Cornell Engineering community, as president of a professional engineering fraternity (Theta Tau) and co-president of Cornell Engineering Ambassadors.

My main goal is to use my technical background to create engaging and meaningful user experiences.

I have a passion for developing product knowledge and design experiences, all through hands-on approaches. In fact, the very website you're viewing now was a side project I started in order to teach myself HTML/CSS and learn how to utilize various web development tools. I'm constantly working to learn and improve, so feel free to give me feedback while you're browsing!
