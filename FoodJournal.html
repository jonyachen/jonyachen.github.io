EAT well, TRAVEL often

Bonding over food is a common denominator that brings people together in every region and culture. My favorite memories of travel are usually those created trying out new dishes at a local restaurant, huddling by a street cart vendor, or crammed elbow-to-elbow in a night market. I also often find myself scrolling through mouth watering food pictures when I'm bored. That is why I decided to create a personal collection of travel souvenirs through my food pictures. At each location, I've created a food photo journal. Enjoy!
